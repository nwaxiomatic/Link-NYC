Join our next and last citywide workshop! We promise, it will be a very special experience.

The workshop will take place over two full days the weekend of February 25 and 26. Your thought and final creations will be displayed on the LinkNYC kiosks and this website!

There are only 18 slots so sign up! We need your name and email to tell you when and where we will meet. We are curious to know a bit about yourself as well, as we are trying to bring together a group as diverse as possible to the workshop.

We will not share any of the information you provide us in this form.

What you’ll be doing:
Together with a group of people, you will think of something that preoccupies you and everyone else in the group. Everyone will have a voice. The workshop follows a simple and effective methodology initially designed at MIT. It’s fun, creative and empowering. The group will articulate their thoughts and come up with cogs, or tiles (think a puzzle in 3D) that represent those thoughts. Once assembled together in a mosaic, the tiles are a tool to foster new ways to constructively approach problems and complexity both visually and collaboratively. We want to hear what you have to say.

Gabriel Kahan, MIT researcher
Antoine Catala, visual artist
