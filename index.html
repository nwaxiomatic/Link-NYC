ABOUT
Understand.nyc is a methodology created to help people from all walks of life to understand and express complex situations. People of all ages, abilities and backgrounds gather in small groups to come up with collective understanding on an environment common to all people in the group. The work is a simple, constructure tool to understand better our everyday lives. The work you see, both on the LinkNYC kiosks around the city, and on understand.nyc, is the product of a collaborative workshop organized around a small group of people who examined what made relationships in NYC so unique.
